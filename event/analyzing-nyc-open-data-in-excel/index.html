Analyzing NYC Open Data in Excel

March 23 @ 1:00 pm - 2:00 pm

Virtual Event
Free
  • « Data Through Design Exhibition – Corpus: BODIES OF DATA
  • Morning Coffee with the Open Data Week Team and NYC Chief Analytics Officer Martha Norrick ☕ 3.24 »

This workshop introduces participants to data analysis in MS Excel using NYC Farmers’ Market data. Learn essential skills like sorting, filtering, and creating visualizations to uncover trends in market locations, operating hours, and SNAP/EBT acceptance. Perfect for beginners looking to organize and interpret public data effectively. The workshop will be held virtually and facilitated by Suporna Saha, Computer Literacy Instructor at Queens Public Library Queensbridge Tech Lab. For any inquiries or concerns, email us at qb-programs@queenslibrary.org.
